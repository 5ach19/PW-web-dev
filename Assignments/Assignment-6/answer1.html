when targeting the same elements with inline, internal, and external CSS, the cascade and specificity rules dictate the order of precedence:
1.Inline styles have the highest precedence and override both internal and external styles.
2.Internal styles take precedence over external styles but are overridden by inline styles.
3.External styles have the lowest precedence and can be overridden by both inline and internal styles.
